# Privacy Policy - Universal Converter

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

## Overview
Universal Converter is a Chrome extension that converts units (weight, length, time, etc.) locally on your device.

## Data We Collect
- **Selected Text**: We temporarily store text you select on webpages in local browser storage to provide instant access in the converter popup.
- **No External Data**: All processing happens locally. We never send your data to any server.

## How We Use Data
- Your selected text is used only to provide unit conversion suggestions.
- Data is stored only in your local browser and is never shared with anyone.

## Data Deletion
All data is stored locally on your device. Clearing your browser data will permanently delete the extension's stored selections.

## Permissions
- **storage**: To cache selected text locally
- **content_scripts**: To detect and process text selections on webpages

## Third-Party Services
This extension does NOT use any external APIs, analytics, or third-party services.
